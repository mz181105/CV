    Judy's Autobiography

    By: Judy Chien

    Hello, my name is Judy. thank you for take time to read my autobiography. About the reason why I want to be an engineer, is about self-actualization. I'm always wandering the meaning of life. No one can live forever, but code can. I want to be an engineer that can solve problem and help people.
    I worked at a small investment company for three years as a administration staff. This job improved my excel skill, and let me realized the convenient of function. This is one of the reason that I want to be an engineer. To let custoner's life more convenient. constructing a web from zero is fun, even if I'm just a beginner. This web is my first work, and it will be better after I learn more.
    It will be a dream come true if I can enter AppWorks School to paratice and work with other elite classmates. But if unfortunately I'm not selected, I will keep learning by myself and try again.
